OUR
SERVICES

At Kick N’ Klean, we offer a wide range of cleaning and care services for your kicks.

BASIC CLEANING

Our Basic Cleaning service is perfect for everyday maintenance. We focus on surface-level dirt and stains to bring back your shoes' fresh and clean appearance.

DEEP CLEANING

For shoes that need more than just a touch-up, our Deep Cleaning dives into every detail. We clean the uppers, midsoles, outsoles, laces, and insoles.

SUEDE & NUBUCK CARE

Delicate materials need delicate care. Our Suede & Nubuck Care service is tailored to protect and restore texture without damaging the material.

PREMIUM KLEAN ESSENTIAL

Our Premium Klean Essential offers a thorough inside-out refresh of your shoes, with odor elimination, plus a free dust bag and silica gel to keep them fresh and protected.

PREMIUM KLEAN: SUEDE & NUBUCK CARE

Our Premium Suede & Nubuck Care gently revives delicate materials with specialized cleaning and soft-brush techniques, with odor elimination, silica gel, and dust bag.
